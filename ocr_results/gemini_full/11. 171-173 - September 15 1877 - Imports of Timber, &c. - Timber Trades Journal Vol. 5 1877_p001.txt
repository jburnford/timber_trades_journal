DUNDEE.
(From our own Correspondent.)
September 13th, 1877.
Trade is on the whole good here at present. The mills are all well engaged, and fair prices are being obtained. Messrs. Wm. Kirkland & Son held their first clearing sale previous to the removal of their extensive premises elsewhere. The company was very large, and good prices ruled. Another sale will be held soon, and the machinery later on. This firm is importing nothing this year.
