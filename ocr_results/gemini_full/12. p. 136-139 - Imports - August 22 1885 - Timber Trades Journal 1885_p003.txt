HERBERT G. WRIGHT.
A meeting of the creditors of Herbert G. Wright, Mr. H. G. Wright, of 10, Palace-chambers, Westminster, engineer and contractor for public works, and formerly of the firm of Wright & Co., ton in 1874, was held on Tuesday at the Bankruptcy Court before Mr. Registrar Hazlitt, one of the Assistant Official Receivers, presiding. The statement of affairs showed liabilities £7,819 15s., and assets nil. The liabilities secured to creditors fully secured are £27,750, expected to rank against the estate for £7,703.
