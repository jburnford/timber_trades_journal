APRIL 26, 1879.] THE TIMBER TRADES JOURNAL. 11

Imports of Timber, &c.
A new and uniform style of reporting imports has been adopted, which, it is thought, will render the list of greater service to our Readers. We now give the name of vessel, port of shipment, general description of cargo, and consignee. This list will gradually be made more complete, and we hope in time to be able to report the arrival of every timber cargo in the United Kingdom. Some abbreviations have been found necessary.
