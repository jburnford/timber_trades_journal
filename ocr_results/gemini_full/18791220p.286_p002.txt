ST. MALO, Dec. 10.-Neptune brig, of St. Malo, from Gothenburg for Rochelle, with deals, drove on the Grande Bouche Rock, on the south coast of this island, during the night of Dec. 8; a steamer endeavoured to get her off, but without success, and it is thought that her bottom is stove.
WARNEMUNDE, Dec. 10.-Caecilie Marie schooner, Graak, from Dantzie for Kiel (sleepers), stranded this morning half a mile east of this place. She had become so encumbered with ice as to be unmanageable, and had therefore to be put ashore. Crew saved, and efforts making to save vessel and cargo.
CADIZ, Dec. 14, 1.20 p.m.-Invincible, British barque, from Pensa-cola for Marseilles (timber), has been abandoned waterlogged, in lat. 31 north, long. 45 west; crew taken off by the Ville de Marseille (French s.), and landed here to-day.
