BORDEAUX.-In its report on the Baltic wood trade, the Echo Forestier says that the situation remains still the same, there being no animation either as regards purchases or sales, and what it says in regard to Bordeaux applies equally to the markets of the North, where pretensions continue to be put forward which are not accepted by the French trade. The only arrival reported is the Hermann from Drontheim. With regard to pit-props, the quotations are 18s. 3d. to Cardiff and from 18s. 6d. to 18s. 9d. to Newport, which represents a decline of 1s. Freights range from 6s. 3d. to 6s. 6d., and business is paralyzed.
