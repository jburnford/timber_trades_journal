SHIPPING INTELLIGENCE.
FREIGHTS.
The last week has shown more activity than has been the case for a considerable period, and a firm tone has been apparent. Although both steam and sailing tonnage has been more plentiful during the last few days, the late period of the year is palpably making charterers desirous of filling their wants, and the demand more than compensates for the increased supply.
