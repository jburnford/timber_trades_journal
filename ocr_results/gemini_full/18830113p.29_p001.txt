TENDERS OPEN.
Additions and Alterations.—Tenders are invited by the committee of the Royal Northern Sea Bathing Infirmary, Scarborough, for various additions and alterations in the present building. The plans can be seen at the offices of the architects, and at the institution, Foreshore Road, Scarborough. Tenders must be delivered on the 22nd inst., to Mr. F. H. Read, 32, Queen Street, Scarborough.
Chapel, &c.—The Ryton Local Board invite tenders for the erection of chapel, lodge, &c., and execution of other works necessary. Full particulars and forms at the offices of Mr. J. J. Lish, Scottish Chambers, Grainger Street West, Newcastle-on-Tyne. Tenders to be delivered to Mr. Henry Dalton, 3, Ashfield Terrace, Ryton-on-Tyne, on 22nd inst.
House, &c.—Persons desirous of tendering for the erection and completion of a dwelling-house and other works for the congregational minister, Totnes, may see plans at Mr. Barron's, Wier View House, Totnes. Tenders not later than the 20th inst. to Mr. Burrow, 15, High Street, Totnes.
School Buildings.—Persons desirous of tendering for the erection of school buildings at the Northumberland Village Homes, Whitley, can see the plans at the offices of Messrs. Oliver & Leeson, Bank Chambers, Moseley Street, Newcastle.
Station.—The directors of the Great Western Railway invite tenders for new passenger station at Blackminster, near Evesham. Plans may be seen at office of engineer at Paddington Station, London. Tenders, addressed to Mr. Fred. G. Saunders, will be received up to the 16th inst.
Wing.—Persons wishing to tender for new wing, consisting of billiard-room, library, studio, gymnasium, &c., at Woodside, Eaglescliffe, for R. H. Appleton, Esq., are requested to forward their names to Mr. H. Weatherill, 59, High Street, Stockton-on-Tees, not later than the 15th inst.
Lodge.—Persons wishing to tender for the construction of a lodge for the Right Hon. Baron Muncaster may notify Messrs. Pickering & Crompton, Whitehaven. Drawings may be perused at the office of the architects until the 22nd inst., on which day tenders will be received.
Market.—The Corporation of Newark-upon-Trent invite tenders for the erection of a market, adjoining the Town Hall. Persons wishing to tender are requested to send their names to Mr. Charles Bell, Dashwood House, 9, New Broad Street.
Mill.—Tenders are invited for various works in re-erection of Sykeing Mill, Earlsheaton. The drawings may be seen at the offices of Messrs. John Kirk & Sons, Dewsbury, up to the 19th inst., on which day tenders are to be delivered.
