THE "GANDY" BELT.
GANDY'S Patent American Cotton Belting can claim a superiority over leather belts in every particular; its strength and gripping power is about double that of the best leather, as shown by repeated tests taken by Kirkaldy, of London. These Tests are given for any length up to 340 feet without joint, to which special attention is directed. These "Gandy" Belts are made of any width up to 60 inches, thus obviating the necessity of having two belts on the same pulley, as it is often the case to have belts of exactly the same tension; hence one or the other is always causing a stoppage. These are avoided by using Gandy's Patent Belts in one width.
