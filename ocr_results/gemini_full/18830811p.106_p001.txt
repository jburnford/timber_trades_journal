ABERDEEN.
Imports here and along the coast have
been comparatively light during the past
month, while the spurt in the building
trades is quieter.
At Messrs. Milne & Co.'s sale last week
of waney pine deals and hard woods
there was a good attendance of buyers,
and prices on the whole were pretty
good. These sales, however, of American
goods are felt to be a necessity in the north,
and are generally well patronized, and value
given for the goods exposed ; with manufactured
stuff this cannot be said. Waney
pine fetched from 2s. 3d. to 3s., according to
girth ; deals, 3s. 2d. to 3s. 6d. ; elm, 2s. to
2s. 4d. ; birch, 2s. to 2s. 2d. ; hickory also
sold well.
