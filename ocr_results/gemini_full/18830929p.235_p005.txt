THE GAZETTE.
FAILURES AND ARRANGEMENTS.
[It must not be overlooked that in many cases these names are published in these lists for their debts, sometimes for the full amount.]
G. A. WILSON.—A petition was filed on Monday last by G. A. Wilson, joiner and builder, of 78, Richardson Street, Stockport. The liabilities are entered at £7,000. The Court appointed Mr. R. R. Shaw, accountant, Manchester, receiver and manager of estate.
