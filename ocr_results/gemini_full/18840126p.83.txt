SHIPPING INTELLIGENCE.
FREIGHTS.
PENSACOLA.—Messrs. Hyer Bros'. latest circular to hand says:—The demand for tonnage for U. K. and Continent is not strong; but we quote as obtainable for medium size vessels hewn timber, 38s. 6d. to 39s.; sawn timber, £5 12s. 6d. to £6; deals, £5 12s. 6d. to £5 15s. The clearances for Great Britain have been : to Liverpool, Florence Chipman, 48,560 ft. sawn, 32,575 ft. lumber; do., Labourer, 5,181 ft. sawn, 39,775 ft. sawn, 28,000 ft. lumber; do., Nordens Dronning, 56,535 ft. lumber; do., Roska, 32,705 ft. lumber; London, Monark, 4,000 ft. sawn, 23,000 ft. lumber; do., Hollin-sawn, 22,000 ft. lumber; King's Lynn, Ungdom, 49,733 ft. sawn, 12,000 ft. lumber; Whitehaven, Madras, 23,096 ft. sawn, 32,000 ft. lumber; Newcastle, Hiram, 21,000 ft. sawn, 21,000 ft. lumber; Gloucester, 36,144 ft. sawn, 23,000 ft. lumber; Cardiff, Carricks, 41,460 ft. sawn, 26,000 ft. lumber; Trinidad, 228,000 ft. sawn, 18 000 ft. lumber; do., Sesti, 52,223 ft. sawn, 36 104 ft. sawn, 18 000 ft. lumber; Greenock, Primo, 20,659 ft. sawn, 49,563 ft. sawn, 34,000 ft. lumber; Dundee, Wellamo, 35,423 ft. sawn, 15,000 ft. lumber.
