TIMBER SALES.
WORCESTERSHIRE.—On Wednesday week Mr. Bentley offered for sale by auction, at the Royal Oak Inn, Broadwas, 15 lots of oak, ash, and elm timber growing on estates in the parishes of Doddenham and Broadwas. There was a numerous attendance of timber merchants. Elm made from 1s. to 1s. 6d. The principal buyers were Messrs. Brewer and Glover (Walsall), Messrs. Hickman & Son (Wolverhampton), Mr. Henry Workman (Woodchester), Mr. Alfred Allen (Worcester), and Messrs. Espley & Co. (Evesham).
