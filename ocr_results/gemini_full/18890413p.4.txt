A parcel of American canary wood sold at 2s. to 2s. 2d., and black walnut 4s. to 5s. 4d., all per cubic foot.
For the mahogany the demand was small, but several lots were placed at 4½d. to 7½d. per superficial foot.
The American white oak planks were cleared out from 2s. 1d. to 2s. 4d. per cubic foot, and for the broad 1st pine deals 3s. 5d. was obtained.
A large variety of Baltic deals and battens, also floorings, linings, and sarkings, were offered, and for all these recent quotations were fully maintained.
No very large quantity of any particular description was placed, but altogether the quantity of goods disposed of was considerable.
