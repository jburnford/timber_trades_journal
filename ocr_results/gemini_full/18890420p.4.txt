SALE OF TREES.—On Wednesday the standing and blown trees on the Cortachy and Airlie estates were disposed of by public bargain in the Airlie Arms Hotel, Kirriemuir. Among the lots sold were the following:— To Mr. Thomas Trichan, Kirriemuir, for £800 ; to Mr. Henry Young, Northmuir, for £285 ; to Mr. Watson, company, at £100. To Mr. Thomas Brisbane, for £395 5s. ; one to Messrs. A. & G. Paterson, for £405 ; one to Mr. Watson, for £695 10s. ; and another to Mr. John Sheepley, at £130.
