WHAT is called "a new religion" has sprung up in Toungu, Burmah—a sort of mixture of Buddhism and Christianity. The founder is a timber merchant, Koh Pai Bah. The disciples keep the Christian Sabbath, and abstain from strong drink. They number several thousands.
