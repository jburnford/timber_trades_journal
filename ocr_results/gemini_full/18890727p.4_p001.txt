DESTRUCTION OF POLMONT SAW-MILL.
FIRE.—On Thursday morning the extensive saw-mill at Polmont Station, and now occupied by Mr. Archibald Paterson, was burnt out to the ground, and the valuable machinery destroyed. The stock of timber in the yard was small, but the total damage is estimated at about £2,000. We understand the property is covered to some extent by insurance. The cause of the origin of the fire is not known.
