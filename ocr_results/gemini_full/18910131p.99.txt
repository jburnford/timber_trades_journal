OPEN CONTRACTS FOR WOOD
FIREWOOD.—For 30 fathoms of very best yellow deal and batten ends for making into firewood, for the Guardians of Mile End Old Town. Forms of tender and full particulars can be obtained on application by letter to Mr. W. Thacker, Guardian's Offices, Bancroft Road, Mile End Road, E.
Tenders will be received until noon on 4th Feb., for ash and hornbeam timber, thickstuff and plank, English elm timber, English oak timber, English oak boat crooks and stems, Canada yellow and red pine, and rock elm timber, Canada yellow pine and spruce deals, mahogany (Mexican and Honduras). Forms of tender, containing conditions of contract and all particulars, may be obtained on personal application at the Contract Department, Admiralty, or by letter addressed "Director of Navy Contracts, Admiralty, Whitehall, S. W."
