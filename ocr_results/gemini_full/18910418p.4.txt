LEITH Ferguson, Davidson, & Co. Tuesday 2nd
EDINBURGH EXHIBITION SALE OF BUILDINGS.—Another of the series of public sales of the timber and other materials was held on Wednesday week, the officiating auctioneer being as usual Mr. J. W. Hardie. There was a large attendance of buyers, and a considerable quantity of goods were disposed of. There was good competition, and the prices realised were similar to the previous sales.
