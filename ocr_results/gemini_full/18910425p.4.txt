FATAL ACCIDENT TO A STUDENT.—James Bryce, student, son of James Bryce, farmer, Francis-field, Perthshire, was fatally injured at Glenalmond Saw-mill on Friday afternoon. The wheel of a cart loaded with wood went into a rut in the road, and while assisting to get it out the binding ropes gave way, and the wood fell upon Bryce and killed him.
