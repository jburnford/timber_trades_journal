IMPORTANT DECISION AS TO DEMURRAGE.
THIS was an action raised by the master and owner of the ss. Aage against Messrs. A. B. Drynan & Co., for £32 as demurrage claimed by them for detention of said steamer in Borrowstounness Dock in September last.
Parties were not agreed as to the sum to be paid, and accordingly an action was raised in the Sheriff Court at Linlithgow, and Sheriff-Substitute Melville, after hearing proof on 9th January last, found the pursuers entitled to a sum of £24, being three days' demurrage and expenses.
