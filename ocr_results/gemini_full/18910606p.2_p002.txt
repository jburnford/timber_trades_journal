COMPANY NEWS.
"LADY ARMSTRONG" STEAMSHIP COMPANY.
Registered in Scotland, with a capital of £32,000 in 640 shares of £50 each, by Messrs. R. & S. Neill, Clark and Orkney writers, Greenock. Object, to purchase, own, and work the ship, to purchase or build any other ship or ships, and to equip and let them out, or trade with the ship, to carry and convey goods, mails, treasure, troops, and passengers, and to carry on the business of shipowners in all its branches.
The first subscribers are:—
Shares.
A. Adam, shipowner, Woodside, Greenock... 1
W. Hamilton, shipowner, Villa Marine, Greenock ... 1
J. M'Intyre, merchant, 51, Newton Street, Greenock ... 1
D. Macdonald, accountant, Wishaw ... 1
W. Watson, spirit merchant, Wishaw ... 1
The managers of the company are to be Messrs. Archibald & Wm. Hamilton, trading as Adam, Hamilton, & Co. Remuneration of 2½ per cent. on all sums earned by the company.
