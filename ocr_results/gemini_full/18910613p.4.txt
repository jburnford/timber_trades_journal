ACCIDENT NEAR DUNDEE.—An accident of a serious nature took place on the Forfar Road, about two miles east of Dundee, on Thursday week. While Mr. A. Wann, builder and contractor, and Mr. A Craig, builder, were returning home after inspecting some quarries the horse shied as they were descending Powrie Brae, and the occupants were thrown out of the vehicle. Mr. Wann is not expected to recover, having sustained serious injuries about the head. Mr. Craig has received internal injuries.
