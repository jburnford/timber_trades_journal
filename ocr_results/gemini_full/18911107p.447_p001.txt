SPANISH EXCHANGE.
BARCELONA.—The exchanges on Tuesday were as follows :—
Sight on London 28.05
90 days on London 27.80
Sight on Paris 10.11 per cent.
MADRID.—The exchange on London on Monday was :—
Paris, 8 days' sight, 27.65 to 27.70 per cent.
London, 90 days' sight, 27.70 per cent.
