SHEARER, JAMES, merchant, Thurso, appeared for his examination, and stated that he began business in 1875 with no capital, and in 1889 he effected composition with his creditors. Besides this, he had carried on shipping, making businesses, in connection with which he kept books. The latter he had kept up, but he only gave up the shipping business it June last. The examination was adjourned for the production of books.
