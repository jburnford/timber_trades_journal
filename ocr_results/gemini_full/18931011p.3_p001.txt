LEITH.
There has been a fair importation of wood goods during the past seven days, and the list of arrivals includes the Martin, with 15,678 battens, the Berthel, with 10,813 deals and battens, and the Hilma, with 16,363 deals and battens, all from Kotka, and all for Messrs. Park, Dobson, & Co ; the Union, from Abo, with 15,780 deals and battens, and the Emigrant, from Skien, with 6,593 deals and battens, both for Messrs. Garland and Roger; the Georg, from Abo, with 9,069 deals and battens, and the Moscow (s), from St. Petersburg, with 32,495 deals and battens, and 1,345 pit props, distributed among various importers.

Prices were, however, firmly maintained, and for the large waney board pine 3s. was got, whilst the smaller sized logs fetched 2s. 4d. to 2s. 8d. per cubic foot. American birch logs brought 1s. 10d. to 2s., and sawn pitch pine 1s. 2½d. Some parcels of fancy woods were also offered, but with the exception of mahogany buyers were wanting.
There was a fair quantity of Baltic and Norwegian deals and battens, as well as floorings, linings, and sashings disposed of at full current rates.
