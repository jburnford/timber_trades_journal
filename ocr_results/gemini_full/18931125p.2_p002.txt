THE METAL TRADES.
Messrs. Henry Pooley & Son (Lim.) have secured from the Clyde Navigation Trustees the contract for a 30-ton weighbridge for Cessnock Docks, and the trustees have also placed with Pooley & Son their contract for a 20-ton weighbridge for Mavisbank Quay.
Messrs. Gillespie, Ure & Co. have commenced business at Springbank Works, New City Road, Glasgow (lately occupied by Thomas Allan & Sons), and will carry on the business of general and ornamental cast-iron founders and workers in wrought iron. While undertaking the general run of iron-foundry work, such as rain-water goods, baths, railings, baths, &c., a special feature will be made of high-class registers, interiors, mantels, and ornamental cast and wrought ironwork for exterior and interior decoration.
Messrs. Brown Brothers & Co., Rosebank Ironworks, Broughton Road, Edinburgh, have just completed the construction of a quantity of machinery for the Chinese Government. It consists of a set of compound pumping-engines with steam accumulators of 150 - horse power and three 10 - ton capstans. The engines will be used for working hydraulic cranes in the arsenal at Foo-chow, and for other purposes as the government extends the hydraulic system. Before the machinery was despatched, it was inspected by Mr. S. Tingfan Chang, secretary to the Chinese Legation, London.
Messrs. C. M. Biggart & Co., ship brokers, 15, Gordon Street, Glasgow, have been appointed representatives in that city for Messrs. Lambert Brothers, home and foreign coaling contractors, London and Cardiff.
The Phosphor Bronze Company, Ltd , have appointed Messrs. Dempster, Moore, & Co., of 49, Robertson Street, Glasgow, as their sole agents in Glasgow for the sale of their manufactures, viz., phosphor-bronze alloys, Duro metal (for bearings), plastic metal and white brass, Babbitt's and other white anti-friction metals, Bull's metals (for propellers, forgings, and stampings), manganese bronze, phosphor tin and phosphor copper. A small stock of some of these metals will be kept at the agents' warehouse, 49, Robertson Street, Glasgow.
