BATES J, builder, Chorley Wood.--At the first sitting of the St. Albans Bankruptcy Court the debtor, whose deficiency is £248, was examined at length. He commenced business in 1894 and has done a considerable business of some size then. Among the creditors were Messrs. Hopkins & Co., Limited, of Wisbech, whose account is £50. The examination was adjourned for the debtor to prepare a full statement of his affairs.
