BUILDING NEWS.
The Glasgow Presbytery of the Church of Scotland have appointed a committee to examine Shettleston (Lanark) Church, which is said to require rebuilding.
* * *
A congregational meeting of St. Paul Street U.P. Church, Aberdeen, has agreed to dispose of the present church to the School Board for £3,000, and erect a new one. It was also resolved to proceed no further in the matter until a site—which it is understood has been selected in Rosemount Viaduct—has been approved by the presbytery.
* * *
The Standing Joint Committee of the Stirling County Council have reported that they had consented to the execution of new sewage works connected with the special drainage district of Camelon, Stenhousemuir, Larbert, and Carron. An application by the Eastern District Committee for consent to the erection of a new fever hospital, and borrowing therefor, has been granted, as also an application by the Western District Committee to borrow a sum of £600 for new pipes in Lennoxtown special water supply district.
