LONDON.
TILBURY DOCK.
THE WATER SUPPLY.—The following paragraph is now going the round of the press:—The influence of forests in protecting the water supply is well illustrated in the case of Greece. In ancient days she possessed immense areas of forest. To-day she has hardly any, and the scarcity of water and injurious climatic effects are traceable to the destruction of the trees.
