JARRAH AT PADDINGTON.—Messrs. Palfre-man, Foster, & Co., 23-35, New Broad Street, E.C., have secured the contract for the supply of Jarrahdale jarrah wood-paving blocks to the Paddington Vestry at £10 12s. per 1,000 blocks.

ASSOCIATION OF CHAMBERS OF COMMERCE.
—The 37th annual meeting of the Association of Chambers of Commerce will be held at the Hotel Metropole on the 9th, 10th, and 11th of March next, under the presidency of Sir Stafford Northcote, M.P. Among the subjects to be dealt with are secondary education, the employers' liability, private Bill legisla-tion, the mail service between England and Ireland, the American mails, Imperial penny postage, and the telegraph rates between Great Britain and France.
