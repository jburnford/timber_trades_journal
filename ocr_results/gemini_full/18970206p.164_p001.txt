A WOOD WORKS AT SKIENSFIORD says THE Vice-Consul at Skienfiord says there are exceptionally good facilities in Porsgrund for establishing ironworks and an iron shipbuilding yard, combined with a dry dock for repairing vessels, and especially steamers, of which there are a great number trading locally, and to others. In Skien there is water-power for about 2,500 h.p. available, by which considerable industry could be developed.

J.H. ROWNTREE
