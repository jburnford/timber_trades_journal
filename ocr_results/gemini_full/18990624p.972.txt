GREENOCK.
There have been no arrivals of wood cargoes this week.
McDowall & Neilsen will hold an auction sale here on Thursday, 29th inst.
The steamers "Janeta" and "Osmansli" are both on their way to Quebec to load timber cargoes for Greenock. The steamer "Ulviken," on the other hand, will load 3,000 tons coal at Greenock for Cronstadt.
The wages paid labourers discharging timber-laden vessels at James Watt Dock is 8d. per hour, with 1s. per hour overtime, and difficulty is experienced in getting men.
The carters in town have made a demand for an increase of 1s. per week. Increased cartage rates will doubtless result.
