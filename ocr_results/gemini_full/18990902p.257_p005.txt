CLARK, ARTHUR, box maker, 11, Little Pulteney Street, W. The first meeting of creditors under this failure was held on Wednesday, before Mr. E. L. Hough, Official Receiver. The chairman reported that an inspector had attended at the debtor's residence, at Balham, and was informed by the landlord that the Sheriff had levied execution at the suit of Messrs. Ltd., and had, on July 8th, sold from £70 or £80, an amount somewhat in excess of the levy. The inspector had also attended at Little Pulteney Street, and seen the debtor's brother who was managing the business. It appeared that the landlord was in possession for half a year's rent. The brother stated that he had not seen the debtor for six weeks, and his disappearance and description had been reported to the police.
