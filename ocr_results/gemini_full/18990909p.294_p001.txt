TRADE REPORTS.
GLASGOW.
The arrivals since last writing have been considerable, and include three full steamer cargoes.
The "Ruthuglen," from Miramichi, has discharged spruce deals and birch planks for Edmiston & Mitchells, and spoolwood for Paisley thread manufacturers.
The "Auroele," from Bangor, with 1,000 standards spruce deals, for Brownlee & Co., Ltd., and Robinson, Dunn & Co. ; the greater part of this cargo is being carted away from ship's side, and the "Marthara," from Miramichi, with 10,000 pieces pine and 70,000 pieces spruce deals, consigned to Edmiston & Mitchells.
By the regular liners from Montreal, the import amounts to 500 standards, the "Sarmatian" having over 150 standards for Edmiston & Mitchells, McDowall & Neilson, and Wright, Graham & Co. ; and the "Amarynthia," with spruce, pine and birch planks, principally for Allison, Cousland & Co. and Calder, Henderson & Livingston.
The import from United States this week is per "Mongolian," from New York—a large parcel of dressed pine boards; and per "Indrani," from Baltimore, a varied assortment of hardwoods, which includes oak and whitewood logs, oak planks and boards, and whitewood boards.
A portion of these are on contract, and a fair amount is already sold of the parcels on consignment.

GREENOCK.
The only arrival here for the week is the "Basuto" (s), 2,497, from Newcastle and Chatham, N.B., which has been berthed at James Watt Dock to discharge 70,329 pieces spruce, deals and ends for McDowall & Neilson, Glasgow; the balance of the cargo, consisting of pine, deals, spoolwood, &c., is to be delivered at Glasgow to several consignees.
Shipbuilders and housebuilders are still well employed, and several new contracts in both departments have recently been concluded.

KIRKCALDY.
KIRKCALDY.—Mr. D. Simpson, joiner, of Kirkcaldy, has secured the contract for joinery work required in the erection of a new secondary school at Burntisland.
The successful quotation was £1,299 11s.
