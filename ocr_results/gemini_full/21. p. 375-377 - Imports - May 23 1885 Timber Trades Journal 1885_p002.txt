FAILURES AND ARRANGEMENTS.

Thomas Henry Scutt.—In the Brighton Bankruptcy Court, on the 6th inst., a receiving order was made against the estate of Thomas Henry Scutt, builder and contractor, of 11, Hassock Road, Preston. The statement of affairs filed by the debtor shows unsecured creditors, £601 6s. 6d.; creditors fully secured £1,850; estimated value of securities, £1,850; creditors to contra, £50; creditors partly secured, £250 10s. 10d.; less estimated value of securities, £250 10s. 10d.; expected to rank for dividend, £651 6s. 6d. The liabilities, £24 7s.; total, £675 13s. 6d. The assets consist of stock-in-trade, estimated to have cost £600, estimated to produce £500; book debts, £69 7s.; total, £569 7s.
