THE LOSS OF THE SS. W. SCHOLTEN.
Among the survivors of the passengers of this ill-fated vessel is Mr. A. C. Hamerton, until recently the representative and measurer of the well-known firm of Messrs. George Shadbolt & Co.
Mr. Hamerton, who has supplied a most interesting narrative of the collision to the daily press, was floating in the water for half an hour when he was picked up by the *Ebro*.
We are sure that his numerous friends in this country will be heartily glad to hear of his fortunate escape.
Mr. Hamerton, who is exceedingly popular in the trade, was proceeding to his home in New York, but we understand that he will now remain in this country till the spring.
