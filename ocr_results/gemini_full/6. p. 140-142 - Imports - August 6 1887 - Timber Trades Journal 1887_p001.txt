DERBY. — Footbridges — For the following works, for the Midland Railway Company: The construction of a timber footbridge at Haworth on the Keighley and Worth Valley Branch, the construction of a timber footbridge near Woodhouse Mill on the Worth Branch; the construction of a retaining wall and widening turnpike road at Heeley. Plans and specifications may be seen, and form of tender and particulars obtained on application to Mr. A. A. Langley, engineer, Midland Railway, Derby, on and after August 3rd. Sealed tenders to be sent in, addressed to the Secretary of the Way and Works Committee, Midland Railway, Derby, by 9 a.m. on 11th August.

GRANTOWN (Scotland).—Villa—For the mason, carpenter, slater, plumber, plaster, and glazier works of a villa to be erected at the West End, Grantown. Plans and specifications may be seen with Messrs. A. & W. Reid, architects, and at the Royal Bank, Grantown, after the 6th inst., and tenders will be received by the architects, at Elgin, up to the 13th inst., at noon.

GREENCASTLE (Co. Down).—Dwellings, &c. —For erection of two new dwellings, out offices, boathouse, enclosure walls, fences, &c., at Greencastle Point, Greencastle, co. Down, for the Commissioners of Irish Lighthouses. Drawings may be seen and specification and form of tender obtained on application at the engineer’s office on any day (Saturday excepted) between 10 a.m. and 4 p.m. Drawings and specifications may also be examined on application at the principal light-keeper’s dwelling, Soldier’s Point, Dundalk; or at the Greencastle Coastguard Station. Bills of quantities can be obtained on payment of 10s. per copy to Mr. O. Armstrong, secretary, Irish Lights Office, Dublin, to whom tenders must be sent by noon on 12th August.

RHOS (near Wrexham). — Chapel.—For erection of a new chapel, for the Committee of the Calvinistic Methodist Chapel (Moriah), Rhos. Plans and specifications to be seen at the house of Mr. E. Smith, near Morrish Chapel. Tenders, sealed and endorsed, to be addressed to Mr. W. Jones, Old Bank Buildings, 43, High Street, Wrexham, by 2 p.m. on the 11th inst.

WALKDEN AND WARDLEY (Lancs.).—Stations —For erection of stations at Walkden and Wardley, near Worsley, for the Lancashire and Yorkshire Railway Company. Stafford, secretary, Manchester.

WESTON (near Bath).—Villas—For erection of a pair of semi-detached villas at Weston, near Bath. Drawing and specification may be seen at the office of Mr. T. B. Silcock, 14, Abbey Churchyard, Bath. Tenders must be delivered by 4 p.m. on 14th inst.
